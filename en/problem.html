The Problem

Global warming is acknowledged as one of the greatest challenges our society faces today.

Approximately 98% of all published climate scientists support the consensus on climate change.

Many people are aware about the problem with climate change and we conducted a survey that shows that a majority want to become more aware of the environmental impact they are causing in their everyday life. This includes the food products they buy.

The public knows we have to lower our emissions to save our planet for future generations. Why not start somewhere where it's easy to make an impact?
Food consumption stands for one fourth of the planets COâ‚‚ emissions. The food industry is enormous. We consume so much food that we have become numb to where the product comes from and how it got on our shelves in our local shop.

If many people do small changes, the impact will be big.
The tools the public have available to know how high their own food emissions are, are not good enough.

We could tell from the survey that the public wants to know their impact. They want to know where they can improve and make the world greener. But just knowing your own emissions doesn't really make you choose greener products. So our next thought was, how could we make people want to lower their emissions?
Thats where MyImpact comes in.
