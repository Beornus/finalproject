Problem

Global warming is a real challenge that our society faces today. Many want to contribute in becoming a more Eco-friendly society, but they cant see how big of an impact their actions make. We want to raise awareness of how much our daily grocery shopping affects our c02 emissions.

We could tell from the survey we created that people wanted to become more aware of how much they affect the climate and the emissions caused by their choices. And also that they would want to compare with other people and friends as motivation to reach a fellow goal as a society. Our replies also stated that many people would scan their receipts to be able to see how they affected the climate, while shopping and so on.

By raising awareness to the people like you and me, of how “green” products are. That would hopefully create a pressure towards the supplier to create more eco-friendly products, because the consumer will hopefully choose the product that has the highest “green” score. Our product will rank items and goods that you can find in your local grocery store. On a scale of 1 to 100. Depending on three categories. We will also have a focus on different ecolabels. We believe that if people can see what differences they make that will motivate them to make more eco-friendly decisions in their day to day life, not only in shopping
