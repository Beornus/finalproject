The Problem

Global warming is one of the great challenges our society faces today. Many want to contribute in becoming a more eco-friendly society, but it’s difficult to know how big of an impact their choices make.

One of humanity’s greatest effects towards the climate, is in how big our food industry has become. We throw away, buy too much and do not think about what effect of what our consume bring to the planet. We have become numb to where the product comes from and how it got to our local shop.

To achieve a better understanding of how relevant this problem is, we created a survey. Theresults from our survey shows that awareness regarding each individual's carbon footprint is low. Over 75% wanted to know more about their impact on the climate.
