LET'S DO IT

Why should you care?

According to a study from The University of Oxford,
24% of all greenhouse gas emissions comes from food production. This is contributing to global warming. There is a significant difference between the food items that leave a small environmental impact and the items that have the most damaging effect on the environment. How can you, the consumer, easily make educated and environmentally friendly decisions while shopping everyday items in the grocery store?

SCAN

Scan the barcode on your receipt with MyImpact’s technology, and get results from your purchase! You can also scan the barcode of the products you want to buy to see what their score is. After scanning you will receive a list of your purchased products along with their respective score.

COMPARE

When inspecting a single product in our database you can see how it scores on emissions, how ecological it is and whether or not the product is sustainable. The product can be added to your purchases, or be compared to another product of your choosing to see which one has the higher score. You will also see similar products with high scores to get inspired to achieve an even higher score on your next trip to the grocery store!

GO GREEN

Your best scores will be displayed on your page together with your achievements. If you are really good about keeping your emissions low, you might even end up on the leaderboards! Try to beat your highest ever daily score, or go for an achievement your friends don't have!
