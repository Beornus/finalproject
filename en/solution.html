Our solution to the problem

MyImpact is a tool for everyone to use in their everyday life to make environmentally friendly choices when grocery shopping.

Scanning your receipts with the app when grocery shopping allows you to track the impact your product choices have on your personal emissions. Each product is given a score based on how eco-friendly it is. You can see your total score, and how it compares to others. Scanning individual products gives you information about those products emissions, animal welfare conditions, and use of pesticides and water in production. This makes it easier to choose the more eco-friendly products.

Your data, your choice.

When you use MyImpact, none of your personal data will be shared with third parties. You have full control over what information you would like to share with other users.

The product scores are calculated from the emission of carbon dioxide and other greenhouse gasses in every step of the production. By raising the consumers awareness about their own emissions, they are able to see how big impact small changes can make. People love to compete, and hopefully that competitive spirit will push consumers to make greener choices. Hopefully companies who sell products with high emissions will want to adapt and lower their emissions. We hope this will affect and push both the consumers and companies to lower their emissions to remain competitive.

Our vision forward

In the future we would like to expand MyImpact even more.

Emissions caused by food choices is a big problem, but another area with huge possibilties is the clothing industry. When MyImpact is up and running we will start working towards including clothing products in our database. The clothing industry has already given positive signals regarding this, and we can't wait to bring you an even more extensive experience!
