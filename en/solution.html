Our Solution

MyImpact is a tool for everyone to use in their everyday life to make more environmentally friendly choices when shopping for items you use daily.

You are able to compare products in the grocery store against each other to find the item with the lowest CO₂ footprint, items with focus on ethical production, the suppliers with the best work in animal welfare and minimum use of pesticides and water.
This way you, the consumer, can contribute to change the way food production is handled all around the world.

To be able to compare the products in the grocery store we are calculating the footprint of carbon dioxide and other greenhouse gasses in every step of the production.
We also take in consideration other major food labels that makes the products rank higher from a social and ethical perspective.

By raising awareness to the consumers about their own emissions, they are able to see how big of an impact small changes can do. People love to compete, and hopefully that competitive spirit will push themselves and others to make greener choices. Companies will notice this, and companies with products with bad scores that consumers avoid will want to change this. By sending their data to us, MyImpact will update the score accordingly. In short, this will affect and push both the consumers and companies to lower their emissions to be competitive.

Your data, your choice.

When you use MyImpact, none of your personal data will be shared with third parties. You have full control over what information you would like to share with other users.

Our vision forward

In the future we would like to expand MyImpact even more.

Emissions caused by food choices is a big problem, but another area with huge possibilties is the clothing industry. When MyImpact is up and running we will start working towards including clothing products in our database. The clothing industry has already given positive signals regarding this, and we can't wait to bring you an even more extensive experience!
